ABOUT US

Finding parking is an everyday struggle for commuters and drivers worldwide. We intend to solve that, first in Davis California, by serving real-time parking availability to all our users. Purpose-built sensors are in development, and will be deployed out to parking lots around the Davis area. We will be coming out with an Android app first, however there are plans in place for an iOS port. We are currently in prototyping phase, but stay tuned for more news!
